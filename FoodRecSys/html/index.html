FOOD RECOMMENDATIONS

Hello,

In this experiment, you will receive some recommendations about RECIPES you can enjoy.
These recommendations are based on your choices as well as on the healthiness of the recipe.
In the following, we will ask you to indicate an ingredient of a recipe that you like (e.g., pasta, chicken, etc.).
Next, you will receive different food recommendations and you will be asked to choose the one you prefer.
This process will be repeated for a starter, a main course and dessert.
Finally, you have to fill in a short questionnaire to indicated why you chose a specific recipe.
The whole experiment will take less than five minutes.
Thank for your work!
