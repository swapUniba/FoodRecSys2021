FOOD RECOMMENDATIONS

Hello,

In this experiment you can receive some recommendations about RECIPE you can enjoy.
These recommendations are based on your choices as well as on the healthiness of the recipe.
In the following,we will ask you to express a keyword, indicating further preferences on the type of meal you want, the type of diet you want to follow and the properties of the recipe.
Next, you will receive different food recommendations and you will be asked to choose the one you prefer.
Finally, you have to fill in a short questionnaire evaluating to what extent each recipe can help you reaching your goals.
The whole experiment will take less than five minutes.
